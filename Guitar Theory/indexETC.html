Ear Trainer

What is the point of this?

While developing my ears I have tried out many different ear training methods, apps, and classes. What I ended up doing is saying fuck all of that and spending most of my time trying to learn songs by ear just by sitting with a guitar and listening to them over and over.

My biggest problem was that I struggled to find songs of the appropriate difficulty. Especially in the beggining. Because of this I decided to put together a database of demos sorted by density of mix and complexity of chord progression.
